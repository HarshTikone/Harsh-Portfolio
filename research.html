AI-Powered Research Paper Assistant: Automating Summarization, Evaluation, and Multi-Guideline Formatting

Presented at: International Conference on Intelligent Computing and Networking (ICICN 2025)

This full-length research paper proposes a complete pipeline that automates the tedious process of summarizing research papers, evaluating content quality, and formatting them to meet different journal guidelines. I built a system that leverages NLP techniques and Python to process academic documents with 95% compliance accuracy. It reduced manual screening time by over 70%, making research workflows significantly faster for scholars and editors.

A Systematic Literature Review on Multimodal Conversational Systems: A Deep Dive into Image-Integrated Chat Generation Methods

Presented at: International Conference on Intelligent Computing and Networking (ICICN 2024)

This systematic literature review analyzed state-of-the-art approaches for integrating images into chatbots and conversational AI systems. I studied multimodal deep learning techniques, data fusion strategies, and model architectures that enhance user engagement.
